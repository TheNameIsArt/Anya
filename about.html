Who am I?

My full name is Anya Rezanova Tandrup.

If you're wondering what kind of sorcery that is my middle name, it's just because I'm half russian.

I'm 21 years old, and live with my boyfriend in a dormitory apartment.

For as long as I can remember, I've loved being creative. I started drawing at a young age, and it just kind of stuck.

Now I study multimedia design, and I for the first time in my life, I'm excited about learning.

What can I do?

Through my education as a multimedia designer, I get to know all the different parts of a design process and its development. So far I have worked with:

- HTML5 and CSS3
- Graphic design and image optimization in Photoshop and Illustrator
- Video editing in Premiere Pro

Besides that, I can draw new designs, take photos and am quite good at creative writing.

Why me?

I'm commited and solution-oriented. I find digital design an exciting way to use my creative energy. The whole process, from idea to development is intriguing.

I am driven by helping, wheter it's helping the customer or a colleague to find the right solution for a given task.
