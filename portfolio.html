Flow 1:
Digital image processing and Adobe Photoshop

Our very first assaignment was to walk around Copenhagen and take pictures. We where think of three words that characterizes us, and then take a picture of it. My words where: Creative, polarized (personality wise) and retro.

Creative

Polarized

Retro

With these three pictures we where assigned to process them in different ways in Adobe Photoshop. We needed to:

  • Remove an element
  • Add an element
  • Adjust the image's lighting conditions or give the image an atmosphere with filters or color tinting
  • Cropping and possibly straightening (vertical/horizontal) the image
  • Format and size: the image must be in 4:3 format and saved so that the size is ≦ 1 M pixels
