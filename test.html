Copenhagen Business Academy

At CphBusiness the semesters are divided into five flows, where the fith flow is the exam.
The first four flows surround different themes.

Digital image processing and Adobe Photoshop Visual concept for digital and printed media Build your first website SoMe video production

Flow 1:
Digital image processing and Adobe Photoshop

Our very first assaignment was to walk around Copenhagen and take pictures. We where think of three words that characterizes us, and then take a picture of it. My words where: Creative, polarized (personality wise) and retro.

Creative

Polarized

Retro

With these three pictures we where assigned to process them in different ways in Adobe Photoshop. We needed to:

  • Remove an element
  • Add an element
  • Adjust the image's lighting conditions or give the image an atmosphere with filters or color tinting
  • Cropping and possibly straightening (vertical/horizontal) the image
  • Format and size: the image must be in 4:3 format and saved so that the size is ≦ 1 M pixels

Flow 2:
Visual concept for digital and printed media

During the second flow we we were assaigned to create a new LP album design for a chosen artist. This assignment had to be done in groups. Besides making an album cover, we had to make a landing page for the album (figma prototype).

On the landing page it should be possible for people to buy the album. The landing page should also link to a spotify page were people could stream the album. Lastly we also had to design a banner for the artist.

We started our preperation with a brainstorm followed by a mind map and a sitemap. In this flow we also learned about PBS (Product Breakdown Structure) and WBS (Work Breakdown Structure), so that we also included. The following things can be viewed here.
